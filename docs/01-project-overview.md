# Project Overview

## Project Name
JosGen Laundry POS

## Business Context
JosGen Laundry is a small family-owned laundry shop currently relying on manual records.

## Main Business Problems
- Manual laundry logbook
- Orders are difficult to search and track
- Expenses are not consistently recorded
- Owner cannot easily verify operator-reported figures
- Cash movement is difficult to reconcile
- Unpaid and partially paid orders are difficult to monitor
- Historical reports are difficult to prepare

## Version 1 Goal
Build the smallest production-ready system that solves the core operational problems without adding unnecessary modules.

## Success Criteria
- staff can record and track orders digitally
- owner can review sales, expenses, cash, unpaid balances, and shift closings
- orders can be searched by order number, name, or contact number
- essential stubs and receipts can be printed
- initial and final laundry details are preserved
- cash and digital payments are clearly separated
- reports use one consistent source of truth
