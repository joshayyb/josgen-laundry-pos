# Owner Dashboard

## KPI Types
- PERIOD
- SNAPSHOT_AS_OF_END_DATE
- CURRENT_BACKLOG

## Date Filters
- Today
- Yesterday
- This Week
- Last Week
- This Month
- Last Month
- Year to Date
- Custom Range

## Period KPIs
- Collected Payments
- Expenses
- Estimated Net Profit
- Orders Received
- Total Kilos
- Total Loads

## Snapshot KPIs
- Outstanding Balance as of selected end date
- Expected Cash for selected closed shift
- Actual Cash for selected closed shift
- Short / Over

## Current Backlog KPIs
- Ready for Pickup
- Open Orders
- Missing Finalization

## Rules
- No hardcoded totals
- Cards and drill-down tables use one source of truth
- Snapshot cards show “As of [date]”
- Backlog cards show “Current”
- Empty states explain no data
