# Printing Specification

## Printer
58mm Bluetooth ESC/POS thermal printer.

## Rules
- Printing is manual
- Save first, print second
- No print-all button
- Prevent rapid duplicate taps
- Show printer status and retry
- Printing must work offline
- Reprints must be logged

## Customer Claim Stub
Include shop name, order number, customer name, received date/time, initial kilo, initial price, paid amount, balance, payment status, and notice that final price may change after checking.

## Laundry Tag / Bag Copy
Include shop name, order number, customer name, received date/time, service, initial kilo, and payment status.

Do not show final load count.

## Final Receipt
Include initial/final kilo, final load count, load breakdown, initial price, adjustments, final price, total paid, balance, release date/time, and released by.

## Shift Closing Summary
Include shift details, starting cash, cash movements, expenses, owner withdrawals, expected cash, actual cash, short/over, notes, and closing timestamp.
