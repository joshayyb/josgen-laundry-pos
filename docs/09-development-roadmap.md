# Development Roadmap

## Milestone 0 — Foundation
Android project, Compose, navigation, theme, domain models, fake repositories, tests.

## Milestone 1 — Local Order Flow
Login placeholder, customer form, new order, waiting, Ayusin ang Labada, processing, pickup, search.

## Milestone 2 — Firebase
Authentication, Firestore, Security Rules, Emulator tests, offline persistence, timestamps, atomic writes.

## Milestone 3 — Payments and Expenses
Payment records, expenses, cash ledger, audit records.

## Milestone 4 — Printing
Bluetooth, ESC/POS, print queue, retry, Claim Stub, Laundry Tag, Final Receipt, Shift Closing Summary.

## Milestone 5 — Shift Closing
Open shift, starting cash, expected/actual cash, short/over, locking.

## Milestone 6 — Owner Dashboard
Date filters, KPI types, reports, CSV export.

## Milestone 7 — QA and Pilot
Unit, integration, UI, offline, printer, and actual-shop testing.
